Did you know?

Discover interesting and lesser-known facts about kittens. These fun facts highlight the unique characteristics and behaviors of these adorable creatures.

  • Kittens sleep for an average of 15-20 hours a day.
  • Kittens have 26 temporary teeth, which are replaced by 30 permanent teeth around 6 months of age.
  • Kittens are born with blue eyes, which typically change color as they grow older.
  • Kittens have a specialized collarbone that allows them to always land on their feet when they fall.
  • Kittens communicate with each other and their mother through a combination of vocalizations, body language, and scent marking.
